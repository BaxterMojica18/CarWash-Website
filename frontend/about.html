Who is BuxTek Inc.?

Founded in 2025, BuxTek Inc. emerged from a simple observation: car wash businesses needed better tools to manage their operations effectively. Our founders, with roots in high-scale software development, saw a gap that was begging to be filled.

We're not just building a dashboard; we're building a growth engine for your business. From independent single-bay washes to major multi-location franchises, we provide the intelligence you need to scale.
